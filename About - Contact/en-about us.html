Rastawood.co started professionally in 2012 by Mr.Ali Khamissi in Tehran Iran and since then we have made many new wooden products. The main product is wooden panel from different section of finest Iranian and overseas woods like maple, walnut , beech wood etc. we've achieved to make very solid and water resistant cutting boards mostly made by beech and maple wood, and we trying to push to the next level and start exporting to Europe, East Asia and Australia. Another interesting product which fully designed and made in Rastawood.co is our precious wooden sinks for luxury bathrooms and kitchens. All the success and improvements is because of our good chairman and manager Mr.Ali Khamissi, his hard work for this company is our passion to keep on going and make new products like wooden table tops, kitchen cabinet doors, security doors, luxury wooden main doors up to chairs and tables. in Rastawood.co we use our knowledge and modernism to make the best product that last longer in your house and with the good taste of choosing unique woods you will have the beauty of wood texture and soul of the nature in your home.
